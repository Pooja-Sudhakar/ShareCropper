The primary aim of ShareCropper is to connect farmers within their locality for the efficient use of their resources. Smallholder farmers buy inputs such as seeds,fertilizers at high retail prices, often missing potential gains that come when dealing in bulk. Around 67 percent of India's farmland is held by the marginal farmers with holdings below one hectare.
